FAQ

SoundLedgr offers a game-changing opportunity to unlock new revenue streams that were previously unavailable. By minting your music as NFTs, you can tap into a thriving marketplace of collectors, investors, and fans who are eager to support your artistic journey. Additionally, SoundLedgr empowers you to engage with your fans like never before. Through multi-signature wallets, you can token-gate exclusive content, merchandise, and experiences.

Creating an NFT of your music is easy. First, sign up for an account with SoundLedgr and complete the verification process. Once your account is set up, navigate to the "Create NFT" page and upload your music file, along with any artwork and metadata that you would like to include. Set your price, list your NFT, and you're all set!
